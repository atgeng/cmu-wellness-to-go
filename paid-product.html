Advil is an over-the-counter medication that contains 200 mg of ibuprofen. It's commonly used to relieve pain from headaches, muscle aches and menstrual cramps, as well as to reduce fever. Advil works by reducing hormones that cause inflammation and discomfort, making it a reliable option for everyday pain relief.

Usage Directions

  • Take 1 tablet every 4 to 6 hours as needed for pain or fever.
  • If pain persists, you may take 2 tablets. Do not exceed 6 tablets in 24 hours.
  • Take with food or milk to avoid stomach upset.

Allergy Warning
