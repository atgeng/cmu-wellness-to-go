Product Instructions


Usage Directions

  • Carefully open to avoid tearing the condom.
  • Pinch the tip to leave space for semen, then roll it down over the erect penis before sexual contact.
  • After use, hold the base while withdrawing, wrap it in tissue, and throw it in the trash.
  • Use a new condom every time.

Allergy Warning

  • Natural rubber latex
